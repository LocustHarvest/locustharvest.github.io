What Does "From Plague to Profit" Really Mean?

Exploring the philosophy behind Locust Harvest's mission.

July 8, 2026 — By Locust Harvest

At Locust Harvest, our tagline — "From Plague to Profit" — is more than just a catchy phrase. It's a philosophy, a framework, and a promise. It encapsulates everything we stand for and everything we help others achieve.

What Does "Plague" Mean?

In our context, a "plague" represents any significant disruption or challenge. It could be:

Personal adversity — illness, loss, failure, or hardship.

Professional obstacles — business failure, job loss, or market disruption.

Societal challenges — economic downturns, food insecurity, or climate change.

We believe that within every plague lies the seed of opportunity. The key is recognizing it and having the courage to pursue it.

What Does "Profit" Mean?

"Profit" isn't just about money — though financial sustainability is important. It means:

Transformation — turning a negative situation into a positive outcome.

Restoration — recovering and rebuilding what was lost.

Legacy — leaving something meaningful behind for others.

Impact — making a difference in the lives of others.

The Journey from Plague to Profit

The journey from plague to profit isn't linear. It's a cycle of phases that every person and organization goes through:

Accumulation — The conditions build beneath the surface, unseen by most.

Manipulation — A period of uncertainty and confusion.

Distribution — Movement becomes clear and recognizable.

Devastation — The plague arrives — the lowest point of the cycle.

Restoration — Recovery begins, and profit is claimed.

Why This Matters

Understanding this cycle helps people and organizations navigate challenges with intention rather than reacting in fear. It provides a framework for resilience and a roadmap for transformation.

At Locust Harvest, we capture and amplify the stories of those who have walked this path — from devastation to restoration, from plague to profit. We believe these stories inspire others, build community, and create lasting change.

Your Story Matters

If you've experienced a plague and emerged with profit — whether financial, personal, or spiritual — we want to hear your story. Together, we can turn disruption into opportunity and inspire a global movement of resilience.

Because every plague has a profit. And every story has the power to change lives.
